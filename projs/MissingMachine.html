Problem definition

We are given a vector identifiers of \( N = 2^d - 1 \) unique binary strings of length \( d \). Therefore one of the \(2^d\) possible identifiers is missing. The identifiers are already sorted (in the increasing order). Our task is to find the missing identifier..

For example:

Input: identifiers = {"000", "001", "011", "100", "101", "110", "111"}
Output: Missing identifier = "010"
