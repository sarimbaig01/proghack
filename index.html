Programming Sessions

  • The problems in these sessions are designed to challenge your problem-solving and algorithm writing skills, as well as your ability to code and test programs.
  • Some problems below are considered more challenging than others and are marked by a '*' and in some cases by more than one '*'s. The number of stars indicates the perceived challenge of the problem.
  • Function stubs and testing code will mostly be in Python, however, the sessions are essentially language neutral.
  • There are no submissions, and no assessments for these sessions.
  • Work at your own pace, at your own leisure.
